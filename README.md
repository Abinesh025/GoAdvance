**Step-by-Step Guide to Build a Digital Marketing**

**Static Website**

**Step 1: Understand the Business Clearly**

- Ask what services are being offered (Social Media Marketing, SEO, Google Ads, Website

Development, Content Creation).

- Identify the target audience (Local shops, Startups, Students, Small businesses).
- Understand what makes the business different from competitors.

**Step 2: Plan Website Structure**

- Home
- About Us
- Services
- Portfolio / Projects
- Testimonials
- Contact

**Step 3: Home Page Content**

- Add a strong headline (e.g., Grow Your Business with Smart Digital Marketing).
- Add a short description about helping businesses grow online.
- Include a Call-To-Action button (Get Free Consultation).
- Add short service overview and Why Choose Us section.

**Step 4: About Page Content**

- Explain who you are.
- Why you started digital marketing.
- Mission and vision.
- Goals of the agency.

**Step 5: Services Page Content**

- Create separate sections for each service.
- Explain what the service is.
- Explain why it is important.
- Explain how you help clients.

**Step 6: Portfolio Page**

- Add sample posts or demo projects.
- Add screenshots with short explanations.
- If no real projects, create 2â€“3 demo case studies.

**Step 7: Testimonials Page**

- Add 2â€“3 positive reviews from friends or early clients.

**Step 8: Contact Page**

- Phone number.
- Email address.
- WhatsApp link.
- Contact form.
- Google Map (if local business).

**Step 9: Professional Process**

- Create content in Google Docs first.
- Get client approval before development.
- Start development only after finalizing content.

**Bonus Tips for First Project**

- Focus on building portfolio over money.
- Deliver clean and professional UI.
- Ask permission to showcase the project in your portfolio.
